# Wikipedia Navigation

## Search
- Search input: `#searchInput` or `#searchform input`
- Search suggestions appear as you type
- Press Enter to search or go to exact match

## Article Structure
- Table of contents at top (collapsible)
- Infobox on right side with key facts
- References at bottom
- "See also" section for related articles

## Navigation
- Language links in sidebar for other languages
- Categories at bottom of articles
- Internal links are blue, external have icon

## Tips
- Use `/wiki/Article_Name` URL format
- Underscores replace spaces in URLs
- Mobile version at `en.m.wikipedia.org`
